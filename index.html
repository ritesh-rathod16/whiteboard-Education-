Diet Plan

7-Day Nutrition Plan

  • Day 1 (Strength Training): Roti + Chicken Curry + Dal + Cucumber Salad
  • Day 2 (HIIT): Quinoa + Moong Dal + Curd
  • Day 3 (Leg Day): Rice + Mutton Curry + Beetroot Salad
  • Day 4 (Upper Body): Roti + Egg Bhurji + Curd
  • Day 5 (Endurance): Brown Rice + Grilled Fish + Lauki Sabzi
  • Day 6 (Recovery): Quinoa + Chana Masala + Cucumber Raita
  • Day 7 (Rest Day): Dosa + Sambar + Flaxseed Chutney

Daily Macros: Protein (1.6-2.2g/kg), Carbs (3-5g/kg), Fats (0.8-1g/kg)

Workout Routine

6-Day Gym Workout Split

  • Day 1 (Chest & Triceps): Bench Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Cable Flys
  • Day 2 (Back & Biceps): Pull-Ups, Barbell Rows, Deadlifts
  • Day 3 (Legs - Quads): Squats, Bulgarian Split Squats, Leg Press
  • Day 4 (Shoulders & Abs): Overhead Press, Lateral Raises, Hanging Leg Raises
  • Day 5 (Arms): Close-Grip Bench, EZ Bar Curls, Triceps Pushdown
  • Day 6 (Legs - Hamstrings/Glutes + HIIT): Romanian Deadlifts, Hip Thrusts, Farmer's Walk

Rest Day: Sunday (active recovery: yoga/walking)

6-Day Calisthenics Plan

  • Day 1 (Upper Push): Push-Ups, Pike Push-Ups, Dips, Handstand Holds
  • Day 2 (Upper Pull): Pull-Ups, Bodyweight Rows, Archer Rows
  • Day 3 (Legs & Core): Pistol Squats, Jump Squats, Nordic Curls
  • Day 4 (Skill & Mobility): Handstand Practice, Front Lever Progressions
  • Day 5 (Full Body HIIT): Burpees, Mountain Climbers, Explosive Push-Ups
  • Day 6 (Freestyle): Muscle-Up Progressions, Human Flag Practice

Rest Day: Sunday (active recovery: yoga/stretching)
